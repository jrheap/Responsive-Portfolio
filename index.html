About me

I was born and raised in West Valley City, Utah. I moved to the south end of the Salt Lake Valley and have been there ever since.
I have been married to Kenzie for a year and a half. no kids yet but we have TWO fur babies, yeah i said two not just one but two! but they are awesome!
The oldest pup's name is Willow, she is a German Shepard. The next is Maverick, he is a German shepard husky mix.

I love the Mountains, whether I am sitting on the mountain side on a cold, brisk opening day of the deer hunt or flying through them on a dirt bike.
I just cant get enough of the Moiutains. Taking the dogs camping is always an interesting adventure.

I try to be out going all the time but it doesnt work all the time...(Mostly i am just awkward) I love learning new things, and love trying to figure out how things work.
You'll often see me staring at the most random thing, i am just trying to figure out how it was built or what makes it work. I already know its weird so its ok judge me on that!
